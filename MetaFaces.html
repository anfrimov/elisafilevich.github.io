Metacognition of facial expressions

We communicate a lot with subtle facial expressions. But we normally don't see ourselves while we're making them. So we wondered how much we know, about the way we look when we make facial expressions.
It turns out, we don't know much! We're writing up the results and hope to publish them soon. We collaborate with Julius Verrel for this project.
